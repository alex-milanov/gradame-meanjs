Какво е Grada.me

We truly believe that every individual can change the world by doing small things and changing their own community. That is why we created a platform for the participation of citizens=heroes in problem solving of their communities, with a focus on removing bureaucratic obstacles that are often daunting for many.



1. Намери проблем

Градски проблеми, които те притесняват

2. Подай сигнал

Чрез мобилното приложение или уеб сайта.

3. Виж

Проблемите отстранени от власите или се организирай със съсеси и отстрани проблмите сам.

Как работи?

The process is really simple. Report an issue using the Heroj app or this website, get as many people as you can to vote for it to get resolved, and see it solved by the local authorities; Platform will handle the boring stuff for you.
